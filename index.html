S J IRON & METALS Co.

S J IRON & METALS Co. is one of the leading scrap metal processing companies in the Middle East, accredited by the Bureau of International Recycling (Gold) and ISRI.

We buy all kinds of electric transformers, copper cables, aluminum scrap, motors, and other non-ferrous metals and export to America, Europe, Korea, and India.
